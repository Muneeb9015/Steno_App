Mission Briefing

Restoring privacy in the digital age through advanced steganography.

Project Architecture

Quantum Stego-AES is a dual-layer security suite designed to bypass surveillance and censorship. While standard encryption scrambles data, this tool takes it a step further by hiding the scrambled data inside innocent images.

It utilizes the Least Significant Bit (LSB) algorithm to inject binary data into the pixels of PNG/JPG files, rendering the secret message invisible to the human eye.
